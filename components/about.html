About Me

Hey! I'm Ronald — I get way too excited about solving problems that actually matter. I've spent the last few years turning conversations into products that reached over 300,000 people across Africa.

My philosophy is simple: listen first, build second, and never stop asking "but why?" I can talk about my years in product management, but what gets me out of bed is the chance to make a real difference.

I'm especially passionate about mental health advocacy. Co-founding an initiative that reached 200,000+ young people taught me that sometimes the most important metrics aren't the ones on your dashboard.

A "failed" product launch once became our most successful feature — after I shut up and actually listened to users.

When I'm not diving deep into Python scripts or getting lost in user analytics at 2 AM, you'll find me exploring how emerging tech can drive real social change. I live for those late-night conversations about what's possible when technology meets genuine human need.

The best products aren't born from boardrooms or eureka moments — they come from real conversations with real people about real problems. And honestly? That's way more fun than any algorithm I've ever written.
